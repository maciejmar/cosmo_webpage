About

Polish journalist, writer, and dedicated human rights activist with a strong commitment to freedom and democracy.

Michal Orzechowski publishes extensively in both Polish and English media, bringing critical issues to international attention. His work spans multiple formats including books, film scripts, and comprehensive human rights reports.

With deep experience working alongside the EU Parliament, Michal has been instrumental in advancing human rights causes, including initiating the Polish Parliamentary Caucus for Tibet.
